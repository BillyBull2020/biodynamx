## THE "360-DEGREE FIREPOWER" AUDIT MANDATE

Every business audit must investigate the "Silent Killers" of revenue. We look past basic website speed and dig into:

1. **Visibility (SEO/AEO/GEO)**
   - Is the brand cited by AI? (Answer Engine Optimization)
   - Do they have Generative Engine Optimization strategies in place?
   - Is there FAQ schema? Without it, LLMs (Gemini, ChatGPT) will not recommend them.

2. **Reputation (GMB & Reviews)**
   - Are there low stars on Google My Business?
   - Are there missing responses to reviews?
   - Are photos outdated? Trust is a strict binary in the AI era.

3. **Conversion (The "Leaking Bucket")**
   - Are calls going to voicemail?
   - Is there a lack of automated follow-up?
   - Do they lack a 24/7 AI agent to capture leads at 2 AM?

4. **Social (Stale Presence)**
   - When was their last post? 2024? Stale social presence is a trust-killer for high-ticket clients.
