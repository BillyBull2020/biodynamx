1. **Reputation (GMB Reviews)**

2. **Call-to-Voicemail Leaks**
   - Are calls going to voicemail? Are there automated SMS/AI follow-ups?

3. **Visibility (SEO/AEO/GEO)**
   - Is the brand cited by AI? (Answer Engine Optimization)
   - Do they have Generative Engine Optimization strategies in place?
   - Is there FAQ schema? Without it, LLMs (Gemini, ChatGPT) will not recommend them.
